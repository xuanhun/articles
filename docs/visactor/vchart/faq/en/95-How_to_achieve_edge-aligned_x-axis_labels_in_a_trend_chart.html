How do I make the x-axis labels stick to the edge of the trend chart?

Problem Description

As shown in the figure, can the label on the leftmost side of the x-axis of the trend chart be aligned with the left border of the chart area, and the label on the rightmost side be aligned with the right border of the chart area?

Solution

  • When the axis type is band, you can eliminate the padding on the left and right sides by configuring trimPadding: true
  • The value of label.flush is true, which means the axis label is indented inward and does not exceed the axis range
